Reservations

Omnia Bistro is a vibrant and elegant restaurant which can host lunch events from 12pm – 4pm or dinner events from 6pm – 10pm. We can comfortably accommodate up to 100 guests for a cocktail event and up to 60 guests for a seated event.

You will have a choice of 4 hour cocktail menu or three, four, five course set menu. We have a vast variety of beverages available for selection. Beverages will be charged on consumption which can include champagne, prosecco, sparkling wine, wine, tap beers, cocktails, spirts and non-alcoholic beverages.

Minimum spends apply for exclusive use of Omnia and is inclusive of venue hire, food and beverage arrangements.

For all other enquiries:

Omnia large group reservations for up to 10 guests, email info@omniabistro.com.au

Events enquiries at Bromley Event Space, email events@lkhospitality.com.au
