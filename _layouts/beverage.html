Philosophy

Yugen offers beverages that showcase the diverse produce of its dishes. A way to explore the intimately intertwined histories of East and West. Discover the depth and breadth of our extensive beverage offering with our Consulting Beverage Manager, Ryan Kimball.

Using preserving, dehydrating, and fermenting techniques that began in Asian cuisine, Yugen marries modern tastes with traditional produce. Shiso vinegar as a drinking shrub. Aromatised plum wine to create a fusion, sweet vermouth. Drinks noted by Japanese citrus in place of traditional lemons and limes.

An offering influenced by history, interpreted for now. Beverages that tell the stories of tiki drinks in Asia, and trace diplomatic relationships. Tastes that admire the past, and look forward to right now.
