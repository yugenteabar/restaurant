The Yugen Experience

Leave the world behind. Descend into a night underground of dining, drinking, and music. Don’t think about where you’re going next, you’re already there. Yugen is an immersive experience that announces the comfort found in what is, and the possibility of what could be. It’s the new way to say dining after dark. A neo-traditional offering influenced by the cultural richness of Asia. Nostalgic tastes told through the contemporary, using produce that isn’t just right, but perfect.

A subterranean setting to heighten the senses. Eight-metre ceilings, industrial interiors with refined details, divided across three distinct areas. A lower dining room offers considered dishes, bold flavours, and late nights. Food made memorable by the sizzling accent of umami, and spice. A six-seat, mezzanine Omakase bar that fuses tradition with instinct. Let go. Take a journey with Head Chef, Alex Yu, to the familiar with a twist. Feel at ease in the warmth of the skilled front of house team led by General Manager, Thibaut Chuzeville.

Celebrate with friends at the ten seat private booth. Take the ultimate vantage point to see the night ahead unfold. Wherever you are amongst us, the night has just begun.

Come. Turn up the volume. Express yourself. We’ll see you underground.
